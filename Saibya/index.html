SAIBYA

UNMANNED GROUND VEHICLE

Mini-sized Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) based robotic platform is designed aiming to provide a versatile and maneuverable robotic solution for tasks that require access to confined, challenging and inaccessible environments. Whether you need exploration, surveillance, remote inspection, search and rescue, environmental monitoring, precision agriculture, security, education, and research, Saibya is your ultimate companion. It offers reduced risk to humans, cost-effective deployment, and the ability to collect data, perform tasks, and navigate spaces that larger vehicles cannot easily accomplish. It can be tailored to meet diverse operational needs and applications.
